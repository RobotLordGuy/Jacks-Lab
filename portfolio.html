// Who I Am

The Mind Behind It

I'm a curious mind at the crossroads of science, technology, and deep time. Whether I'm digging through fossil records, designing circuits, writing algorithms, or dreaming up the next big invention — every project starts with one question: What if?

I believe dinosaurs are the greatest unsolved mystery in biology, that code is the closest thing we have to magic, and that DNA is the most elegant program ever written. My work tries to connect all of it.
